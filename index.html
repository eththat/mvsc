Mountain View Smart Contracts

Mountain View Smart Contracts is a cooperative research group, established in April 2016, in Mountain View, CA.

We meet weekly to develop smart contracts.

Our recent activities:

We designed, built, and supported the deployment of the World Food Programmes building blocks project before handing it off to a much more capable team. We are currently conducting research in creating new connections between peer-production and markets. This work is sponsored, in part, by Mozilla.

Occasionally, we will share our experiences in our blog - Coding With Gas

The State of Smart Contract Development


In this series, we build a reliable set of tools and techniques for smart contract development. Each installement of the series will have all tutorial steps tested, before it is published. We will release an update about once a year.
