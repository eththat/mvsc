Mountain View Smart Contracts

Mountain View Smart Contracts is a cooperative research group, established in April 2016, at an undisclosed location in Mountain View, CA.

We meet weekly to develop smart contracts.

In our blog, Cooking With Gas, we will share our experiences as we learn to develop and deploy practical smart contracts in this nascent space.
