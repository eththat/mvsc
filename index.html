Mountain View Smart Contracts

Mountain View Smart Contracts is a cooperative research group, established in April 2016, in Mountain View, CA.

We meet weekly to develop smart contracts.

In our blog, Coding With Gas, we will share our experiences as we learn to develop and deploy practical smart contracts in this nascent space.

Smart Contracts: The Working Parts

A Tutorial Series on Smart Contract Development


In this series, we build a reliable set of tools and techniques for smart contract development. Each installement of the series will have all tutorial steps tested, before it is published.
